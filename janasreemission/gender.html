Gender Mission

To facilitate discussion on gender sensitive development approach as well as to facilitate action research in this area, JSDM has initiated gender mission under the directorship of Dr. Gita Gopal. Gender Mission focuses on

  • Gender workshops at State, District, Block and Panchayat levels for SHG leaders, Elected Members of PRIs, NGO leaders so as to create an understanding of the gender related issues in Kerala and facilitate a discussion.
  • An institute for gender studies, training and research to facilitate gender related studies.
  • Skill building and setting up of counselling centre in Panchayats led by trained counselors

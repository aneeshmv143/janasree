Janasree

Janasree Mission is a movement to facilitate sustainable development through people's participation irrespective of caste, religion, creed or sex. It intends to uphold the Gandhian principles of self sustainable development. A mission movement, which can ensure the participation of common public in the developmental initiatives and which can extend the outcome of the scientific, technological, social and economic developments, uniformly to the common public, is the need of the hour.

Jansree Mission is :
  • A Vision- about self sufficient sustainable Kerala, about the impact of collective action, about actualizing of dreams of Mahatma Gandhi, about grass root response to intellectual and cultural invasion.

  • A Movement- constantly growing, of people from all walks of life based on the principles of Peace, Purity and Prosperity.

  • A Way of Life- adopting sustainable practices in all their actions and becoming role models in the society

  • A Commitment - a life-long commitment to the principle of sustainability and dedicated action.

  • A Participatory Action- a collective effort in achieving the results envisaged.

  • An Inclusive Movement- engaging all sectors, all issues, all people, using appropriate strategies, and partnering with all institutions.

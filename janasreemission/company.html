Janasree Substainable Development Mission(JSDM)

"Janasree Sustainable Development Mission, a registered Non-Governmental Organisation (NGO) under Travancore Cochin Scientifc, Educational, Literary and Charitable Societies Act XII of 1955, is a well structured organisation with its head quarters at the state level with tiers at the District, Block and Mandalam extending up to the grassroots at the ward level. JSDM has a State Level Unit and 14 district level units, each of which are registered as separate societies. Over the past years Janasree Mission has grown as one of the biggest organisations in Kerala in terms of people's participation with over 60000 Sangams which encompass an approximate beneficiary participation of 10 lakh people.

Since inception, the organisation is working for overall development through social mobilization and critical intervention in livelihood of the people through its 15 missions.


Agriculture Mission
: Self sufficiency in food crop production; Toxin-free food production
Gender Mission
: Research & action on Gender-sensitive development
Health Mission
: Quality Healthcare services for all through trained healthcare personnel and institutions at grassroots
Entrepreneurship Development Mission
: Promoting Microenterprises; Capacity development on establishing, sustaining& marketing in microenterprise
Consumer Mission
: Consumer awareness generation and grievance redressal
Energy Mission
: Energy conservation; promotion of alternative (conventional and non-conventional) energy sources
Cultural Mission
: Protection of cultural heritage; promotion of literary/library movements; promotion of cultural exchange programmes and festivals
Environment Mission
: Conservation, protection and restoration of natural resources, promote green technology and prevent environment degradation
Housing Mission
: Low-cost housing; promoting use of local resources
Legal Mission
: Legal literacy; Legal protection and awareness
Labour and employment Mission
: Generation of employment opportunities; Skills & Capacity development; ensuring accessibility to employment programmes
Social Justice Mission
: Equal rights & opportunities for all; Creating awareness on constitutional & social rights; mainstreaming the marginalized
NRI Mission
: Programmes for the welfare and inclusion of NRIs
Science and Technology Mission
: Promoting & adopting scientific knowledge & technological innovations
Tribal Mission
: Socio-economic, political & legal protection to tribal community

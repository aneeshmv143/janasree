Janasree Sustainable Development Mission


Janasree Mission is a movement to facilitate sustainable development through people's participation irrespective of caste, religion, creed or sex. It intends to uphold the Gandhian principles of self sustainable development.

A vision

about self sufficient sustainable Kerala, about the impact of collective action,about actualizing of dreams of Mahatma Gandhi,about grass root response to intellectual and cultural invasion.

A Movement

movement of people based on the principles of Peace, Purity and Prosperity. People from all walks of life unconditionally join the movement and it will grow each day.

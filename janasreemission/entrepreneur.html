Entrepreneur Development Mission

Entrepreneurship Development Mission(EDP) mission envisages identifying and designing appropriate enterprises, promotion of various enterprises, creating awareness on sustainability of enterprises, provide training on value addition of products, arrange for marketing and management training, quality improvement in production, moulding entrepreneurs through training and skill building, promotion of micro enterprises,, generating employment opportunities and conduct research for technological advancements in value addition and processing.

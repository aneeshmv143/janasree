Projects

Following projects are currently being implemented

Toxin Free Food Production

JSDM has taken strides in moving towards the dream of self sustainability in food crops with focus on toxin free methods for Kerala. During the year 2011-12 a total of 783 Janasree groups in Kerala have initiated group farming of vegetables and plantain in land area varying from 1 to 4 hectors. In 73000 families we have facilitated cultivation of vegetables in the available land. In this process a total of 7800 ha land which was kept uncultivated has been turned in to food crop cultivating areas.

Jaivasree Project

JSDM has launched this unique project during the Jaivasree festival of 2011 at Trivandrum. The project is expected to be undertaken in partnership with Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, MKSP, Horticultural Mission, Agriculture Department, State Bank of Travancore, Jaivasree Toxin Free Food Products and Producers Company and other likely minded organisations. Project envisages transforming Kerala in to self sustainability in food crop cultivation within a span of 5 years.

Jaivasree Festival

National seminar and exhibition is organised every year as an effort to promote toxin free food production. Exhibition focussed on organic products, cultivation practices and equipments. In 2011 a total of 230000 people attended the programme. Seminar and discussions on various issues relating to food security and toxin free cultivation practices is also organised as part of the festival.

Gender Equity Programme

As an initiative to facilitate discussion on gender sensitive development approach as well as to facilitate an action research on gender aware approach in development initiatives, JSDM has started a gender mission under the directorship of Dr. Gita Gopal. Gender Mission has already initiated the following activities

  • Gender workshops at State, District, Block and Panchayat levels for SHG leaders, Elected Members of PRIs, NGO leaders so as to create an understanding of the gender related issues in Kerala and facilitate a discussion. The training is an ongoing process.

  • Developing a state specific monitoring framework to assess the gender aware approach in development interventions.

  • An action research involving 50000 thousand SHGs over a period of 5 years to assess the impact of development initiatives already initiated by government and non-government actors. Already developed monitoring framework will be used for action research.

  • An institute for gender studies, training and research to facilitate gender related studies.

  • Launched a dedicated website to disseminate gender mission activities and e-group to share experiences and learning of the filed level experimentation.

Micro Finance Programme

Micro Finance programme of JSDM is implemented through a dedicated NBFC established for the same. District Units have been promoted in all 14 districts and membership of Sanghams is steadily progressing. In 12 out of the 14 districts lending activity also has been initiated. Kottayam and Trichur districts are yet to start lending activity. A total of Rs. 599.582 has been disbursed to 12 MBTs so far. A total of 614 Sanghams have been assisted with loan. Loan amount per SHG is ranging from 50,000 to 100,000. Majority of the loan has been utilised for agricultural purpose and a few joint business ventures.

Consumer Awareness programmes

Kerala being a prominent consumer state awareness and action regarding the rights of consumer, remedial mechanisms established by government and other agencies, process for grievance redressal measures etc is important. JSDM has consumed this role and established a mission to design and implement consumer awareness programme. Regular awareness campaign is being carried out to educate consumers about their rights and initiate legal action wherever necessary.

Energy Conservation Initiatives

One of the major issues for future Kerala is energy requirement. JSDM has formed energy mission to spearhead grass root level action with participation of people for energy conservation and promotion of alternative energy sources. Motivation for Rural families to adopt alternative energy sources has been given. Linkage with various government subsidy and technical assistance was facilitated to install equipments for the same in rural households.

SHG Formation and Strengthening

A key activity focused by JSDM is formation and strengthening of Janasree Sanghams (SHGs). There are 60190 sanghams spread across the state with a membership strength of 1078000. Sanghams have been formed in all the Panchayats in Kerala and a 6 tier mechanism for monitoring and guidance of the Sanghams has been developed. Supervisory and monitoring systems are placed at Sangham, ward, Panchayat, Block, District and State levels so as to ensure standardization of activities as well as to check deviation from common guidelines issued.

Capacity Building programmes

JSDM focuses on conducting training and providing techno managerial inputs at various levels to ensure that the personnel and volunteers are adequately enabled to lead programme implementation. We have conducted 7 state level programmes, 14 district level training programmes, 113 block level training programmes, 312 Panchayat level training programmes and 2872 ward yogam level organised meetings in which 138458 people participated and undergone knowledge and skill enhancement programmes. In addition to this we also facilitate distribution of IEC materials, exposure programmes, e - group for information dissemination etc. are facilitated as part of capacity building initiative.

Leadership Development For Minority Women

Janasree organized the leadershipdevelopment training for 350 women spread across the state in 14 districts.The implementation of the programme was undertaken by the district branch offices of Janasree. Under the supervision of the Janasree central office based in Trivandrum, each district office selected 25 beneficiaries,women belonging to the Muslim and Christian communities for the training.
